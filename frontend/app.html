Welcome to ADLYTICS

ADLYTICS analyses your ad copy and video scripts before you spend a single naira — predicting performance, detecting weaknesses, and showing you exactly how to fix them.

  • 📊19-tab analysis covering hook strength, credibility, emotional pull, CTA, personas, and more
  • ✨Rewrite engine that fixes weak ads and shows before/after scores
  • 🛡️Compliance scanner for TikTok, Meta, and Nigerian regulations
  • 💰Budget simulator in your local currency (₦, GH₵, £, $)
🔢

Understanding your scores

Every ad gets 8 scores from 0–100. They are calculated using a weighted formula — hook and credibility carry the most weight.

81–100: Excellent
61–80: Good
41–60: Average
0–40: Poor
💡 Hook (25%) and Credibility (20%) have the biggest impact on your overall score. Fix these first.
✍️

Getting the best results

Paste your actual ad copy or video script — not a brief or idea. The more specific your input, the more accurate the analysis.

  • ✅Use the Video Script field for TikTok/Reels — it analyses hook delivery, timecodes, and speech flow separately
  • ✅Select the correct industry — personas, hooks, and compliance rules all change per industry
  • ✅Set the right country — all budget figures appear in your local currency
  • ⚡After analysis, use Rewrite Engine (Overview tab) to fix weak spots with one click
